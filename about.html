ABOUT US

WHAT WE DO

Founded in 2007, MSS Security originally began operating in Manchester’s Northern Quarter providing Door Supervisors to the stylish and unique vibrant venues.

By developing as a distinguished security provider, MSS are now operating across Manchester and the rest of the UK offering extensive security packages tailored to the client’s needs.

MSS Security now provide security personnel to more venues than any other security provider in the Northern Quarter. This is a claim we are very proud of and we feel it reflects our commitment to the area and understanding of the venues and their customers. Based in the Northern Quarter MSS are committed to the area and actively promote and raise awareness of this great community encouraging people to shop, drink and do business.

The main provision of MSS Security is and always has been specialising in providing Door Supervisors to the leisure and entertainment sector, but as the company developed in size and reputation throughout the industry we have expanded to meet the demand for more services and products.Manchester and the rest of the UK
