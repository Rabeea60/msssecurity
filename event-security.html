EVENT SECURITY

Our event teams are all trained in NVQ level 2 Spectator Safety as a minimum, with Supervisors and senior Management level 3.

Event security & stewards for all occasions from large music festivals, concerts to co-operative events endeavouring in making the event a success, ensuring safety is not compromised.

Customer Safety is our main objective when delivering event security, all event personnel wear our standard uniform and are fully equipped with all PPE attire complying with Health and Safety legislation. A professional high profile appearance is also what you and all the people attending will see whilst making them feel safe and welcome to the event.

Our event teams are all trained in NVQ level 2 Spectator Safety as a minimum, with Supervisors and senior Management level 3. Additionally our operatives are customer service trained to NVQ level 2 as a minimum.

All our event teams have a minimum two First Aid Responders as well as two Event Supervisors to ensure your event runs as smooth as possible.

We can help with the initial planning including all risk assessments, method statements, security plans, evacuation procedures and risk management.
