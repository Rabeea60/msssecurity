OUR SERVICES

FACILITIES MANAGEMENT

Commercial cleaning services across the North West.

Security Facility & Management provide commercial cleaning services across the North West. All of our cleaners are thoroughly vetted, employed by us and DBS checked.

We provide all cleaning materials and only provide cleaners with experience and proven reliability and punctuality. All cleaning products are of high quality and in stock. You can specify to use your own cleaning products if you prefer.

All cleaners are uniformed and carry identification cards for verification purposes. We hold full public liability insurance for up to £10,000,000.

We use a time and attendance system which we can report write from for your peace of mind. Our cleaners use a clocking in system which reports their real-time presence at your premises, building or location.

One of our Senior Managers will visit your site prior to engaging in our services to establish your needs. All cleaners will then be inducted formally by this Manager.
