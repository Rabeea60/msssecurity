CAR PARK MANAGEMENT

Event car parking services

We can manage your car park be it on a ad-hoc basis or under contract. All Supervisors, Stewards and Security Officers will be in full uniform and high visibility clothing.

We will guide vehicles to their bays and patrol the car park for the duration of the event. Fees will be collected and securely stored.

All our operatives are trained in Spectator Safety Level 2, First Aid at Work, Customer Service and Conflict Management. So you can be assured any disputes will be handled quickly and professionally.

We can provide signage and digital vehicle counters to record the number of vehicles onsite each day.
